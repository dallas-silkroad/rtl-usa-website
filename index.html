OEM model · For new builds & smart platforms

Secure delivery, seamlessly built in.

A patented delivery vault engineered to disappear into the home — embedded in door systems, walls, and smart-home platforms. Scan. Drop. Secure. It's that simple, and it's ready to carry your brand.

How it works

Scan. Drop. Secure.

01 · SCAN

Verified at the door

The driver interacts with an intuitive drop-down door. The built-in barcode scanner and HD camera verify and record each delivery, so every drop-off is authenticated in real time.

02 · DROP

One-time secure access

Once scanned, the system unlocks a one-time access hatch sized for standard parcels and guides the package into a protected compartment — no codes, no fuss.

03 · SECURE

Locked & logged

The vault locks automatically. The homeowner is notified instantly in-app and retrieves the package conveniently from inside the home.
